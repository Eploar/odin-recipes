Olivier Salad

Description

Olivier salad is a traditional Balkans starter. It is a cold, creamy salad made with diced potatoes, carrots, peas, eggs, pickles, and sometimes chicken or ham, all mixed together with mayonnaise. The texture is chunky but soft, and the flavor is a mix of mild, slightly tangy, and creamy. It’s simple but very filling. it actually originated in 19th-century Moscow, created by a chef named Lucien Olivier, which is why it is also called Russian Salad
